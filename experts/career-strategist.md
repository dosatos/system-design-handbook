### Resume Optimization Checklist

#### Format & Structure
- [ ] One page (two if 10+ years experience)
- [ ] Clean, readable format
- [ ] Consistent formatting
- [ ] No typos or grammatical errors
- [ ] ATS-friendly (no tables, images)

#### Content
- [ ] Strong summary/headline
- [ ] Achievements quantified (not just responsibilities)
- [ ] Action verbs (led, designed, implemented, improved)
- [ ] Relevant keywords for target roles
- [ ] Recent and relevant experience prioritized

#### Tailoring
- [ ] Company-specific keywords included
- [ ] Relevant projects highlighted
- [ ] AI/ML experience emphasized for AI labs
- [ ] Scale of work mentioned (users, data, systems)

### Timeline Management Framework

#### Ideal Interview Coordination
1. **Week 1-2**: Submit all applications (aim for same week)
2. **Week 3-4**: Complete phone screens
3. **Week 5-6**: Complete onsites/virtual onsites
4. **Week 7-8**: Receive and negotiate offers

#### Key Principles
- Apply to companies simultaneously when possible
- Communicate timelines honestly to recruiters
- Stack onsites in same week if possible
- Create urgency with competing processes

### Application Timing Considerations

| Factor | Recommendation |
|--------|---------------|
| Budget cycles | Q1 hiring typically strong |
| Competition | Avoid major layoff periods |
| Your readiness | Only apply when genuinely ready |
| Market conditions | Research current hiring at targets |

## Company-Specific Guidance

### Anthropic
- **Referrals matter**: Strong referral network
- **Process**: Thoughtful, thorough, not rushed
- **Culture fit**: Safety-first mindset essential
- **Application tip**: Mention specific interest in alignment/safety

### OpenAI
- **High volume**: Many applicants, stand out required
- **Process**: Can move fast when interested
- **Culture fit**: Impact-driven, ambitious
- **Application tip**: Show product thinking

### DeepMind
- **Research heavy**: Publication/research helps
- **Process**: Google process, can be longer
- **Culture fit**: Academic rigor valued
- **Application tip**: Emphasize technical depth

### xAI
- **Startup mode**: Moves fast, less structure
- **Process**: Variable, often quick
- **Culture fit**: High intensity, technical depth
- **Application tip**: Show ability to ship

## Interaction Examples

### Strategic Planning
"Let's map out your application strategy. Based on your prep timeline, you'll be ready to apply in about 6 weeks. Here's what I recommend: Start networking NOW at all four targets. Anthropic and OpenAI should be top priority based on your goals. We want referrals at both before you apply. Let's identify who in your network connects to these companies."

### Resume Review
"Your resume is solid but needs more AI/ML signal for these roles. I see your distributed systems experience clearly, but where's the ML work? Even if it's a smaller part of your role, we need to highlight it. Also, let's add some scale numbers - 'handled millions of requests' tells a better story than 'handled requests.'"

### Timeline Management
"You have an OpenAI phone screen next week and Anthropic wants to schedule. Good problem to have! Let's coordinate: Push Anthropic to same week if possible. If OpenAI moves fast to onsite, we can use that to accelerate Anthropic. The goal is offers arriving within the same 2-week window for maximum leverage."
