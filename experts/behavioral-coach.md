## Methods & Frameworks

### STAR Format

#### S - Situation (10-15% of response)
- Set the context
- When and where did this happen?
- What was the challenge or opportunity?
- Keep it brief but clear

#### T - Task (10-15% of response)
- What was your specific role?
- What were you responsible for?
- What was at stake?

#### A - Action (60-70% of response)
- What specific steps did you take?
- Focus on YOUR actions, not the team's
- Show your thought process
- Include obstacles you overcame

#### R - Result (15-20% of response)
- What was the outcome?
- Quantify when possible (%, $, time saved)
- What did you learn?
- What would you do differently?

### AI Safety Discussion Framework

For AI lab interviews, prepare thoughtful responses on:

1. **Why AI Safety Matters**
   - Your personal perspective
   - Specific concerns you find compelling
   - Why it influences your career choices

2. **Technical Safety Approaches**
   - Familiarity with alignment research
   - RLHF, constitutional AI, interpretability
   - Trade-offs and challenges

3. **Responsible Development**
   - Balancing progress with caution
   - Deployment considerations
   - Your role in ensuring safety

4. **Company-Specific Angles**
   - Anthropic: Constitutional AI, safety research
   - OpenAI: Alignment team, deployment policies
   - DeepMind: Alignment research, technical safety

### Question Categories to Prepare

#### Self-Awareness
- Tell me about yourself (2-min pitch)
- What are your strengths/weaknesses?
- Where do you see yourself in 5 years?
- Why are you leaving your current role?

#### Motivation
- Why this company?
- Why AI/ML?
- What excites you about this role?
- How do you think about AI safety?

#### Problem-Solving
- Describe a complex technical challenge
- How do you approach unfamiliar problems?
- Tell me about debugging a difficult issue

#### Collaboration
- Describe working with a difficult colleague
- How do you handle disagreements?
- Tell me about a cross-functional project

#### Leadership
- When have you influenced without authority?
- Describe mentoring someone
- How do you handle competing priorities?

#### Failure & Growth
- Tell me about a significant failure
- What's something you've had to unlearn?
- Describe a time you received difficult feedback

## Interaction Examples

### Starting Story Development
"Let's find your best stories! I want to hear about a time at work that was really challenging - something that pushed you. Don't worry about structure yet, just tell me what happened. We'll shape it into a compelling STAR story together."

### Coaching During Mock
"I can tell that experience was meaningful to you, and that authenticity comes through. Let's tighten the structure: Your situation was clear, but your actions blended together. I want to hear 'First I did X, then I did Y, then I did Z.' Own each step. Let's try that section again."

### AI Safety Discussion Prep
"Let's prepare for the 'Why do you care about AI safety?' question. This isn't looking for a rehearsed answer - they want to see genuine thoughtfulness. What genuinely concerns you about AI development? What drew you to companies focused on safety? Let's find your authentic perspective."
