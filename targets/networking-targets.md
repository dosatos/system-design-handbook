## Contact Finding Strategy

### Where to Look
1. **1st-degree connections**: LinkedIn search by company
2. **2nd-degree connections**: Ask for introductions
3. **Alumni networks**: University alumni at companies
4. **Tech communities**: Twitter, conferences, meetups
5. **Blind/forums**: Anonymous outreach (careful)
6. **Cold outreach**: LinkedIn or email

### Ideal Contact Profile
- Works in relevant team/function
- Has been there 1+ years (can refer)
- Active on LinkedIn (responsive)
- Shared background (school, past company)

### Quick Reference

**Cold LinkedIn**:
```
Hi [Name], I noticed [specific observation]. I'm preparing for
[role] at [Company]. Would you have 15 min to chat about your
experience? No pressure if not - I appreciate you considering!
```

**Warm Introduction Request**:
```
Hi [Mutual], would you be comfortable introducing me to [Target]?
I'm interested in [Company] and would love to learn about their
experience.
```

## Conversation Goals

### In Each Informational Interview

**Learn**:
- What's day-to-day like?
- What does success look like?
- What's the interview process?
- What advice for interview prep?

**Build Relationship**:
- Show genuine interest
- Ask thoughtful questions
- Offer value if possible
- Be respectful of time

**Set Up Referral**:
- End with clear next step
- Ask if comfortable referring
- Make it easy for them

### Questions to Avoid
- Asking for referral in first message
- Asking about compensation directly
- Making it all about you
- Questions easily Googled

## Networking Metrics

### Response Rates
- Cold outreach: Expect 20-30%
- Warm intro: Expect 50-70%
- Alumni connection: Expect 40-50%

### Conversion Goals
- Outreach → Response: 25%+
- Response → Conversation: 80%+
- Conversation → Referral ask: 100%
- Referral ask → Referral: 50%+

---

## Thank You Protocol

### After Informational Interview
- Send thank you within 24 hours
- Reference specific advice they gave
- Share how you'll apply their advice
- Keep door open for future

### After Referral
- Send heartfelt thank you
- Update them on application status
- Let them know outcome (good or bad)
- Offer to help them in return

### After Getting the Job
- Personal thank you to all who helped
- LinkedIn recommendation if appropriate
- Pay it forward to others

## Network Maintenance

### Even After Job Search
- Stay connected with contacts
- Engage with their content
- Offer help when possible
