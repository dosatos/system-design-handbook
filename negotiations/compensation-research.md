## What to Negotiate

### High Flexibility
1. **Signing Bonus**: Most flexible component
2. **Start Date**: Can impact bonus payouts
3. **Equity Shares**: Often more flexible than base

### Medium Flexibility
4. **Base Salary**: Usually 5-15% room
5. **Level/Title**: Impacts long-term trajectory
6. **Remote Work**: Policy dependent

### Low Flexibility
7. **Vesting Schedule**: Usually standardized
8. **Benefits**: Usually standardized
9. **PTO**: Usually standardized

## Things to Consider Beyond TC

### Financial
- Equity vesting cliff (typically 1 year)
- Refresh grant philosophy
- Bonus structure and reliability
- 401k match
- Tax implications of equity type

### Non-Financial
- Team and manager quality
- Technical challenge level
- Growth opportunities
- Work-life balance
- Remote flexibility
- Company trajectory

---

## Red Flags in Offers

### Watch For
- Unusually low equity percentage
- Aggressive vesting cliffs (>1 year)
- Unclear equity valuation methodology
- Pressure to decide quickly (<1 week)
- Unwillingness to put offer in writing
- Significant disconnect from market data

### Questions to Ask
- What's the current 409A valuation?
- What's the refresh grant philosophy?
- How are levels determined?
- What's the path to next level?
