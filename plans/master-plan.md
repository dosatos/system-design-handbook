# Master Plan: 12-Week Interview Preparation

## Overview

A comprehensive 12-week preparation program targeting Senior SWE roles at Anthropic, OpenAI, DeepMind, and xAI.

**Start Date**: [To be set]
**Target Interview Window**: Weeks 10-12
**Weekly Time Commitment**: 20-25 hours

## Phase Summary

| Phase | Weeks | Focus | Key Milestones |
|-------|-------|-------|----------------|
| 1: Foundations | 1-3 | Core skills, patterns, fundamentals | 75 problems, DDIA 1-6 |
| 2: Intermediate | 4-6 | Depth, systems, RLHF | 50 problems, ML systems |
| 3: Company-Specific | 7-9 | Targeted prep, applications | 40 problems, apps submitted |
| 4: Interview Mode | 10-12 | Mocks, active interviewing, negotiation | Offers secured |

---

## Phase 1: Foundations (Weeks 1-3)

### Goals
- Establish strong coding fundamentals
- Build pattern recognition
- Start ML and systems foundations
- Begin behavioral story development

### Coding (75 problems)
- Week 1: Arrays, Two Pointers, Sliding Window (25 problems)
- Week 2: Stack, Binary Search, Linked Lists (25 problems)
- Week 3: Trees, Tries, Heap/Priority Queue (25 problems)

**Reference**: `roadmap/02-technical-preparation/leetcode-strategy.md`

### System Design
- Week 1: DDIA Chapters 1-2, URL Shortener design
- Week 2: DDIA Chapters 3-4, Rate Limiter design
- Week 3: DDIA Chapters 5-6, Notification System design

**Reference**: `roadmap/05-system-design/framework.md`

### ML Fundamentals
- Week 1: Transformer architecture deep dive
- Week 2: Attention mechanisms, positional encoding
- Week 3: Training fundamentals, loss functions

**Reference**: `roadmap/02-technical-preparation/ml-fundamentals.md`

### Behavioral
- Week 1: Audit work history for stories
- Week 2: Draft 3 core STAR stories
- Week 3: Practice "tell me about yourself"

**Reference**: `roadmap/06-behavioral/star-framework.md`

### Career
- Begin company research
- Update LinkedIn profile
- Start identifying network connections

---

## Phase 2: Intermediate (Weeks 4-6)

### Goals
- Tackle harder problem types
- Deep dive into ML systems
- Understand RLHF pipeline
- Refine behavioral stories

### Coding (50 problems)
- Week 4: Graphs (BFS/DFS), Backtracking (20 problems)
- Week 5: Dynamic Programming (20 problems)
- Week 6: Advanced patterns, company-tagged (10 problems)

### System Design
- Week 4: DDIA Chapters 7-9, Chat System design
- Week 5: ML Training Pipeline design, Feature Store
- Week 6: Model Serving Infrastructure, Recommendation System

**Reference**: `roadmap/05-system-design/ml-infrastructure.md`

### ML Deep Dive
- Week 4: RLHF pipeline end-to-end
- Week 5: Constitutional AI, reward modeling
- Week 6: Inference optimization, KV caching

### Behavioral
- Week 4: Complete story bank (8-10 stories)
- Week 5: AI safety discussion preparation
- Week 6: Company culture research

### Career
- Active networking (5+ conversations)
- Begin resume tailoring
- Identify referral opportunities

## Phase 3: Company-Specific (Weeks 7-9)

### Goals
- Target specific companies
- Submit applications

- Mock interview practice

### Coding (40 problems)
- Week 7: Anthropic/OpenAI tagged problems (15)
- Week 8: DeepMind/xAI tagged + hard problems (15)

### System Design

- Week 8: Full mock design interview #2

### ML

### Behavioral
- Week 7: Full mock behavioral interview #1
- Week 8: Full mock behavioral interview #2
- Week 9: Full mock behavioral interview #3

### Career
- Week 7: Submit Anthropic application
- Week 8: Submit OpenAI, DeepMind applications
- Week 9: Submit remaining applications, follow up

---

## Phase 4: Interview Mode (Weeks 10-12)

### Goals
- Active interviewing
- Maintain sharpness
- Secure and negotiate offers

### Daily Routine
- Morning: 2-3 coding problems (warm-up)
- Midday: System design or ML review
- Evening: Company-specific research for next interview

### Interview Activities
- Phone screens as scheduled
- On-site/virtual on-site interviews
- Follow-up and thank you notes

### Negotiation
- Week 10: Review compensation research
- Week 11: Practice negotiation scripts
- Week 12: Active negotiation as offers arrive

### Maintenance
- Continue 1-2 problems daily
- Review difficult topics before interviews
- Get adequate sleep and manage stress

---

## Weekly Structure

### Weekday Template
- **Morning (1-2 hrs)**: Coding practice
- **Evening (2-3 hrs)**: System design, ML, or behavioral
- **Total**: 3-5 hours/day

### Weekend Template
- **Saturday (3-4 hrs)**: Deep work (hard problems, system design)
- **Sunday (2-3 hrs)**: Review, planning, mock interviews
- **Total**: 5-7 hours/weekend

### Weekly Total: 20-25 hours

## Adjustments

This plan should be adjusted based on:
- Interview scheduling (accelerate if interviews scheduled early)
- Weakness identification (spend more time on weak areas)
- Company feedback (adjust focus based on recruiter guidance)
- Life circumstances (maintain sustainable pace)

Review and adjust weekly during Sunday planning sessions.
