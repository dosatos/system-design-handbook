## Interview Day Protocol

### Before Interview
1. Wake up early, healthy breakfast
2. 30-min warm-up: 1 Easy + 1 Medium problem
3. Review company notes (15 min)
4. Review your resume and stories (10 min)
5. Test setup (camera, mic, IDE, whiteboard)
6. Take 5-10 minute break before interview

### During Interview
1. Breathe and stay calm
2. Clarify before jumping in
3. Think out loud
4. Ask for hints if stuck
5. Leave time for questions

### After Interview
1. Write down all questions asked
2. Note your performance honestly
3. Identify areas to improve
4. Send thank-you email (same day)
5. Update interview tracker

## Feedback Loop

### After Each Interview, Ask:
- What went well?
- What could improve?
- Any unexpected questions?
- How was my communication?
- Did I manage time well?

### Adjust Preparation:
