## Negotiation Execution

### When Offer Arrives
1. **Express genuine enthusiasm**: "I'm thrilled to receive this offer"
2. **Ask for written details**: "Could you send the full offer details in writing?"
3. **Request time**: "I'd like a few days to review carefully"
4. **Don't commit**: Even if it's great, take time

### Counter-Offer Strategy

**Step 1: Prepare Your Ask**
- Base salary increase (5-15% above offer)
- Equity increase (if significant portion of TC)
- Signing bonus (often most flexible)

**Step 2: Deliver Counter**
"Thank you again for this offer. I'm very excited about joining [Company]. After reviewing the details and considering my experience and market data, I was hoping we could discuss [specific asks]. Is there flexibility here?"

**Step 3: Handle Response**
- If yes: Great! Get in writing, then accept
- If partial: Evaluate if acceptable
- If no: Decide if you'll accept as-is

## Negotiation Scripts

### Leveraging Competing Offer
"I want to be transparent - I have another offer from [Company] that's quite competitive. I'm genuinely more excited about [Your Company] because [specific reason], but the compensation gap is significant. Can we discuss closing that gap?"

### Asking for More Equity
"The base salary looks good. For the equity component, given my experience and the market data I've seen, I was hoping for something closer to [X shares]. Is there flexibility there?"

### Asking for More Signing Bonus
"I understand the base and equity may be at the top of the band. Would you be able to increase the signing bonus? I'm giving up [X] in unvested equity at my current company."

### Final Push
"I really want to make this work. If we can get to [specific number], I'm ready to sign today."

## Acceptance and Decline Communications

### Acceptance
"I'm thrilled to accept the offer to join [Company] as [Title]. Thank you for your confidence in me and for the thoughtful negotiation process. I'm excited to start on [Date]. Please send over the final paperwork."

### Decline (After Competing Offer)
"Thank you so much for the offer to join [Company]. After careful consideration, I've decided to accept another opportunity that aligns more closely with my current goals. I really enjoyed meeting the team and learning about your work. I hope our paths cross again in the future."
