## Energy and Stamina

### For Full-Day Interviews
- **Breakfast**: Protein-rich, avoid sugar crash
- **Between rounds**: Quick walk, deep breaths
- **Lunch**: Engage with team, ask good questions
- **Afternoon slump**: Energy from good questions, not caffeine
- **End of day**: Thank everyone, strong close

### Mental Strategies
- Each interview is a fresh start
- Past rounds don't define future ones
- Treat interviewers as future colleagues
- Be curious, not just performative
- It's okay to not know everything

## Offer Timeline Management

If offers start coming:
1. Express enthusiasm and gratitude
2. Ask for details in writing
3. Request reasonable deadline (1-2 weeks)
4. Coordinate with other processes
5. Don't accept immediately

### Timeline Communication Script
"I'm very excited about this offer and [Company] is at the top of my list. I have a couple of other processes wrapping up in the next [X days]. Would it be possible to have until [date] to make my decision? I want to be fully committed when I say yes."
