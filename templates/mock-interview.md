## Scoring Guide

### 5 - Excellent
- Would be a strong pass in real interview
- Demonstrated expertise
- Communicated exceptionally well
- Handled all challenges smoothly

### 4 - Good
- Would likely pass
- Solid performance with minor issues
- Good communication
- Handled most challenges well

### 3 - Borderline
- Could go either way
- Decent performance with some gaps
- Communication adequate
- Struggled with some aspects

### 2 - Needs Work
- Would likely not pass
- Significant gaps in knowledge/skill
- Communication issues
- Struggled with multiple aspects

### 1 - Not Ready
- Would not pass
- Major gaps requiring significant work
- Fundamental communication issues
- Unable to make progress
