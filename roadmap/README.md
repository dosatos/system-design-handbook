# Senior Software Engineer Interview Preparation Runbook
## Top AI Companies: OpenAI, Anthropic, DeepMind, xAI

**Duration:** 8-12 weeks
**Target:** Senior Software Engineer (5+ years experience)
**Last Updated:** January 2026

---

## Overview

This runbook provides a comprehensive, week-by-week preparation guide for landing a Senior Software Engineer position at top AI companies. It's based on extensive research of current job postings, interview experiences, and company resources.

### Target Companies

| Company | Focus | Culture |
|---------|-------|---------|
| **OpenAI** | AGI development, ChatGPT, GPT models | Fast-paced, mission-driven |
| **Anthropic** | AI safety, Claude, Constitutional AI | Research-oriented, safety-first |
| **DeepMind** | Fundamental AI research, Gemini | Academic, long-term thinking |
| **xAI** | Universe understanding, Grok | Startup velocity, first-principles |

### Time Commitment

- **Daily:** 3-4 hours
- **Weekends:** 4-6 hours for projects
- **Mock interviews:** 2-3x per week (Weeks 7+)

## Preparation Phases

| Phase | Weeks | Focus |
|-------|-------|-------|
| **Foundation** | 1-3 | Data structures, algorithms, ML fundamentals |
| **Intermediate** | 4-6 | Advanced algorithms, system design, LLM infrastructure |
| **Company-Specific** | 7-9 | Company-style problems, behavioral prep, AI safety |
| **Interview Mode** | 10-12 | Mock interviews, active interviewing, negotiation |

---

## Success Metrics

By the end of this program, you should be able to:

- [ ] Solve LeetCode medium problems in 20-25 minutes
- [ ] Solve LeetCode hard problems in 35-45 minutes
- [ ] Design ML systems end-to-end in 45-minute sessions
- [ ] Articulate each company's mission and values
- [ ] Discuss AI safety trade-offs intelligently
- [ ] Have 10 polished STAR stories ready
- [ ] Complete 2+ portfolio projects demonstrating relevant skills

---

## Getting Started

1. **Assess your starting point** - Take a baseline LeetCode assessment
2. **Choose your target companies** - Read profiles in `01-company-research/`
3. **Build your schedule** - Block 3-4 hours daily for preparation
4. **Start Week 1** - Follow `02-technical-preparation/phase1-foundations.md`
5. **Track progress** - Use templates in `08-weekly-milestones/`

**Good luck with your preparation!**
