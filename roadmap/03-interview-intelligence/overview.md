## Process Comparison

| Aspect | OpenAI | Anthropic | DeepMind | xAI |
|--------|--------|-----------|----------|-----|
| **Avg Duration** | ~30 days | ~20 days | 43-120 days | ~54 days |
| **Coding Difficulty** | Very Hard | Medium-Hard | Medium | Hard |

## Common Interview Stages

### Stage 1: Recruiter Screen (All Companies)
- Duration: 30 minutes
- Purpose: Background, motivation, role fit
- Not technical, but important

**Tips:**
- Research the company beforehand
- Have clear answers for "Why [Company]?"
- Ask about the interview process
- Be enthusiastic but genuine

### Stage 2: Technical Screen

| Company | Format | Duration |
|---------|--------|----------|
| OpenAI | CoderPad live coding | 60 min |
| Anthropic | CodeSignal assessment | 90 min |
| DeepMind | 2 coding + 1 ML fundamentals | ~3 hours |
| xAI | Online Assessment | Varies |

### Stage 3: Onsite/Final Loop

| Company | Rounds | Focus Areas |
|---------|--------|-------------|
| OpenAI | 4-6 over 1-2 days | Coding, System Design, Behavioral, Research |
| Anthropic | 4 rounds in 1 day | Coding, System Design, ML Theory, Behavioral |
| DeepMind | Multiple days | ML Design, Team Lead, People & Culture |
| xAI | 4-5 rounds | Coding, System Design, Behavioral |

- Progressive problem-solving

### DeepMind

- Debugging skills

### xAI

## Key Success Factors

1. **Technical Preparation**
   - Master patterns, not just problems
   - Practice under time pressure
   - Know ML fundamentals

2. **Communication**
   - Think aloud clearly
   - Structure responses
   - Ask good questions

3. **Mission Alignment**
   - Research company values
   - Have genuine interest
   - Connect your story to their mission

4. **Safety Awareness**
   - Understand AI risks
   - Know company's approach
   - Show thoughtfulness
