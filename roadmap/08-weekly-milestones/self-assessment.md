## Pre-Interview Checklist

### 1 Week Before

- [ ] Mock interview scores 4+/5 consistently
- [ ] All 10 behavioral stories practiced
- [ ] Company research complete
- [ ] Resume reviewed and polished
- [ ] Questions for interviewers prepared

### Day Before

- [ ] Light practice only (2-3 easy problems)
- [ ] Review company values and recent news
- [ ] Review your stories
- [ ] Prepare logistics (links, resume, water)
- [ ] Get 8+ hours of sleep

### Morning Of

- [ ] Light breakfast
- [ ] 1-hour coding warm-up
- [ ] Review company-specific notes
- [ ] Arrive/login 10 minutes early
- [ ] Deep breaths, you've prepared!
