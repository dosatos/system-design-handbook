## Culture Fit Indicators

### You'd thrive at OpenAI if you...
- Want to work on cutting-edge AGI products
- Move fast and handle ambiguity well
- Balance safety with shipping
- Care about developer experience

### You'd thrive at Anthropic if you...
- AI safety is a personal priority
- Value research-oriented culture
- Appreciate low-politics environment
- Want to publish and contribute to field

### You'd thrive at DeepMind if you...
- Have strong academic/research background
- Think in long-term research horizons
- Enjoy interdisciplinary collaboration
- Want Google resources + research freedom

### You'd thrive at xAI if you...
- Thrive under pressure
- Value speed over process
- Take initiative without being asked
- Want startup equity + impact

## Application Strategy by Company

### OpenAI
- **Best for:** Product-minded engineers, infrastructure specialists
- **Prepare:** Practical coding, ML system design, mission alignment
- **Referral:** Important but not essential

### Anthropic
- **Best for:** Safety-conscious engineers, independent thinkers
- **Prepare:** Progressive coding challenges, AI safety knowledge
- **Referral:** Values independent work (blogs, open source) highly

### DeepMind
- **Best for:** Research-oriented engineers, PhD holders
- **Prepare:** ML fundamentals, debugging, research discussions
- **Referral:** Research connections help

### xAI
- **Best for:** High-velocity engineers, startup-minded
- **Prepare:** Hard LeetCode, concurrency, system design
- **Referral:** Connections to Musk network valuable

## Recommended Order of Application

### For Risk-Averse Candidates
1. DeepMind (longest process, most forgiving)
2. Anthropic (clear process, fair evaluation)
3. OpenAI (high bar, practical focus)
4. xAI (fast-paced, high pressure)

### For High-Confidence Candidates
1. Start with less-preferred companies for practice
2. Apply to preferred companies after 1-2 interviews
3. Try to align final rounds for negotiation leverage
