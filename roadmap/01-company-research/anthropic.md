## Company Culture

- **AI Safety First:** Primary emphasis on safety and interpretability
- **High-Trust Environment:** Low politics, transparent culture
- **Research + Engineering:** "Engineers do research, researchers do engineering"
- **Retention:** 80% employee retention rate (industry-leading)
- **Long-Term Benefit Trust:** Unique structure ensuring mission endurance

### Culture Quote
> "High-trust, low-politics environment where transparency is fostered and employees feel empowered to voice concerns directly to leadership."

## What Makes Anthropic Different

> "About half our technical staff had no prior ML experience; about half have PhDs, but plenty of brilliant colleagues never went to college."

> "If you've done interesting independent research, written a thoughtful blog post, or contributed to open source, put that at the TOP of your resume."

**Key Insight:** Direct evidence of ability matters more than credentials.

## Interview Tips Specific to Anthropic

1. **AI Safety is woven into every round** - Show you care about implications
2. **Speed and correctness over Big O** - They care about working code fast
3. **Prepare for progressive coding challenges** - 4 levels, increasing complexity
4. **No hidden test cases** - Interviewee-friendly format
5. **Read Alignment Science Blog** - Discuss Constitutional AI, interpretability
6. **Independent work matters** - Blog posts, open source, side projects

## Sample Interview Questions

### Coding
- Build an in-memory database: SET/GET/DELETE → filtered scans → TTL → compression
- Implement bank application with multiple transaction types
- LRU cache with *args and **kwargs handling

### System Design
- "Design a distributed search system for billion documents at million QPS, with LLM inference at 10K+ RPS"
- "Scale a token-generating function to handle 100,000 requests per second"

### Behavioral
- "Why is AI safety important to you personally?"
- "Describe a time you prioritized doing the right thing over the easy thing"
